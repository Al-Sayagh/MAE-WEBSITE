Diplômes & parcours

Degrees & journey

Après un Bachelor of Science en sciences infirmières à l’Université de Floride, Mae poursuit un Master of Science en communication stratégique à l’Université Columbia. Cette double formation allie rigueur scientifique et art de raconter des histoires et l’a amenée à écrire pour le journal étudiant The Independent Florida Alligator sur des sujets culturels et artistiques.

After earning a Bachelor of Science in nursing at the University of Florida, Mae pursued a Master of Science in strategic communication at Columbia University. This dual education combines scientific rigor with the art of storytelling and led her to write for the student newspaper The Independent Florida Alligator on cultural and artistic topics.

Licences & pratiques

Licenses & practice

As a registered nurse licensed in New York, Florida and California, she understands compliance, ethics and care delivery. This clinical experience helps her build marketing strategies that respect patients and regulations.
